Hi, my name is Nursultan. I am a Senior Machine Learning Research Engineer at Yandex, working in the core search team on a system serving more than 100 million monthly active users. My work focuses on research and engineering for large-scale search systems. Currently, I work on LLMs, deep learning, and ranking models for search.

Alongside my work, I am pursuing a BSc/MSc at Innopolis University, a research-oriented institute in Innopolis, Russia.

Previously, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Avito, as a quantitative researcher at a hedge fund, and as a researcher at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence, Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology, Innopolis University, and Saint Petersburg State University.

My primary interests are search and other large-scale algorithmic systems. More broadly, I am interested in quantitative research, deep learning, robotics, and neuroscience. Within machine learning, my research interests include efficient deep learning and distributed systems and cognitive AI/AGI research.

In my free time, I enjoy good coffee, sports, and traveling.
